Blind CSRankings.org Explorer

Blindly explore US universities sized by the publications of their faculty in the selected venues within the specified time period. Clicking on a bubble (which corresponds to a university) loads a stacked bar chart of the faculty (only their initials are shown) that made the publications, broken down by the number of publications at each venue. Clicking on a bar opens a new tab with the corresponding faculty member's website, revealing their identify and the university they belong to.
